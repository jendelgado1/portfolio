about me

Jennifer Delgado is a journalist living in her hometown of Chicago.

As a child, she read avidly, collecting short stories, fashion magazines and every single book from The Baby-sitters Club series. Her passion for words and writing began in her high school speech class.

After graduating from the University of Iowa, Jennifer interned at the Chicago Tribune. Since then, she's worked in various roles at the paper, covering local government, education and breaking news.

In her spare time, Jennifer catches up on reading, runs and travels the world. She's also learning to cook. You can check out photos of her latest culinary experiment on Instagram.
